FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to get an automation live?

Most automations are live within 2–4 weeks. Simple integrations can be done in under a week. Complex multi-system workflows may take 6–8 weeks. We give you a clear timeline before we start.

Do I need to have technical knowledge?

Not at all. We handle everything technical. You just need to tell us what you want to achieve — we design, build, integrate, and train your team to use it.

What tools and platforms do you work with?

We work with all major platforms — HubSpot, Salesforce, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Slack, WhatsApp Business, Zapier, and more. We integrate with whatever you already use.

How much does it cost?

Every engagement is scoped individually based on your needs. We offer project-based pricing and monthly retainers. Book a discovery call and we'll give you a clear estimate — no hidden costs.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

Yes. All our implementations include a 30-day post-launch support period. We also offer ongoing maintenance retainers for clients who want continuous optimisation and monitoring.
